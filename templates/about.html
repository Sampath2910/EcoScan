Our Mission

PeerConnect is dedicated to leveraging technology to solve the global waste crisis. Our AI model provides instant, accurate waste classification to encourage better recycling habits at home and to help local communities manage their resources efficiently.

The Technology

Our classification engine is built using a **ResNet18 convolutional neural network**, trained on thousands of images of common household waste items, achieving a final validation accuracy of over **91%**.
